Our Pool

Locals Only

Locals Only is the first surfable wave pool on the west coast. Nestled in Oregon's scenic Willamette valley, we aim to give surfers the training grounds they deserve so that next time it's double overheard, and offshore angels are singing, you have no doubt that you can handle whatever the sea throws at you.
