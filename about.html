About:

 Locals Only was started by two bestfriends and surfers from the pacific northwest. It's no secret that this region of the world has no shortage of what we might refer to as "challenging" conditions. After a few too many wind blown, finger-numbing, rip-current filled sessions these two surfers started dreaming of a safe haven. A place where the sun always shined and the waves crisp. Thus, the idea was born. Many years and millions of dollars in funding later, we are proud to bring Local's Only to the central vallley of Oregon. To good rips, better friends, and endless surfing!

Locals Only

Locals Only is the first surfable wave pool on the west coast. Nestled in Oregon's scenic Willamette valley, we aim to give surfers the training grounds they deserve so that next time it's double overheard, and offshore angels are singing, you have no doubt that you can handle whatever the sea throws at you.
