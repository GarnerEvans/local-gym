The Oregon Surfers Dream

A wave pool for surfers, by surfers.

Beginner

Small waves, long interval.
Perfect for a first time at the pool.

Intermediate

Head high waves with a 10 secnod interval
Ideal for for progressing from a beginner surfer.

Expert

Overhead waves with less predictable interval.
If you're looking to get barreled or a bit scared, this is the place for you.

Locals Only

Locals Only is the first surfable wave pool on the west coast. Nestled in Oregon's scenic Willamette valley, we aim to give surfers the training grounds they deserve so that next time it's double overheard, and offshore angels are singing, you have no doubt that you can handle whatever the sea throws at you.
